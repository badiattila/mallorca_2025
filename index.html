More About Mallorca

Explore hidden coves, taste local cuisine, and hike the Tramuntana mountains. Mallorca offers something for everyone, from adventure seekers to those looking for relaxation.

7-Day Mallorca Itinerary

  1. Day 1 – Palma de Mallorca
    Explore La Seu Cathedral (EUR9, 1h), Bellver Castle (EUR4, 1.5h), Es Baluard Museum (EUR6, 1.5h), and the Old Town.
    Travel: Palma Airport to City Center ~20 min by taxi (~EUR20).
  2. Days 2–3 – Serra de Tramuntana
    Drive Ma-10 route. Visit Sóller, tram to Port de Sóller (EUR7, 30min), Fornalutx, Deià, Valldemossa (EUR5 monastery entry).
    Stay 1 night in Sóller or Deià (~EUR120–180/night).
    Car rental ~EUR45/day. Drive time ~1h from Palma to Sóller.
  3. Day 4 – Cap de Formentor & North
    Drive to Cap de Formentor (1h 20min). Visit lookout points and Platja de Formentor.
    Explore Pollença or Alcúdia old towns (free). Fuel cost approx EUR15.
  4. Day 5 – East Coast Beaches & Caves
    Swim at Cala Mesquida or Cala Agulla. Visit Cuevas del Drach (EUR16, ~1h guided tour).
    Drive time from north coast: ~1h 15min. Fuel: ~EUR15.
  5. Day 6 – Southern Beaches
    Es Trenc beach (EUR7 parking), Cala Pi, and Cala Llombards.
    Drive ~50min from Palma. Budget for snacks/lunch EUR20.
  6. Day 7 – Palma or Countryside
    Relax at Portixol or cycle through vineyards near Binissalem.
    Optional wine tour: ~EUR25–40. Bike rental ~EUR20/day.

Top Experiences

  • Swim at Es Trenc Beach
  • Visit the historic Palma Cathedral
  • Cycle through the Serra de Tramuntana
  • Sample ensaimada in a local bakery
  • Discover the Caves of Drach
